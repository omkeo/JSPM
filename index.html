Admissions

How to apply

Follow the steps below to start your journey with JSPM. For any help, reach out to our admissions team.

Contact Admissions

1) Check Eligibility

Verify program‑wise criteria (scores, prerequisites, and required documents).

2) Register Online

Fill the application form and upload documents. Keep payment details ready.

3) Entrance & Counseling

Appear for applicable entrance exams and participate in centralized counseling.

4) Seat Allotment & Fees

Confirm your seat by paying fees and completing document verification.

What scholarships are available?

Merit‑based, need‑based, and government scholarships are available. Category and EWS benefits as per norms.

Is hostel facility available?

Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls with mess, Wi‑Fi, study rooms, and 24×7 security.

Can I apply before getting my exam results?

Yes. Provisional admission is possible subject to submission of final mark sheets at the time of enrollment.
